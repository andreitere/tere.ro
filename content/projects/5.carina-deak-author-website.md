A personal website crafted for Romanian author **Carina Deak** to showcase her literary work and connect with readers.

## About the Project

The site serves as the online home for Carina's debut book *"Eternitate în zile numărate"* (Eternity in Numbered Days) — a story about imperfection, healing, authenticity, and courage.

### Features

- **Author biography** — presenting both the person and the writer
- **Book presentation** — dedicated section for "Eternitate în zile numărate"
- **Events calendar** — upcoming and past literary events and book launches
- **Photo gallery** — visual journey through the author's literary path
- **Contact integration** — social media links and direct contact options

### Technical Details

Built with **Astro** for optimal performance and SEO. The design emphasizes elegant typography and a warm, literary aesthetic that reflects the emotional depth of Carina's writing.
